A Note From Richie
Dad,

I just want to tell you how much I love and appreciate you—not just today, but every day. I thought of you this morning, and the first memory that came to mind was all those times you’d pick me up from school on the Harley. From the times you put me on the back of the bike, to the little lessons and the big adventures, you’ve shown me what real freedom and heart look like.

And Dad, when you ride, do it with 100% and with safety—think of me too, and let that give you an extra reason to ride safe every mile. I know soon enough we’ll be riding side by side again—just like old times on the electric scooters, only this time, on real motorcycles.
With all my love,
Richie
